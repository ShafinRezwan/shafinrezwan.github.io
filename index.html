Abloh-ism

A collection of meaningful quotes I reflect on often, from one of my favourite artists; Virgil Abloh

01

"Question Everything"

"Everything I do is for the 17-year-old version of myself."

Challenging conventional wisdom and approaching design with a beginner's mindset. This philosophy emphasizes the importance of maintaining curiosity and questioning established norms in pursuit of innovation.

02

"Tourist vs. Purist"

"In the end we're all tourists, and that's okay."

Celebrating the value of both expert knowledge and fresh perspectives. This principle encourages designers to maintain their curiosity while developing expertise, finding balance between innovation and tradition.

Citation (MLA): Abloh, Virgil. Abloh-Isms. Edited by Larry Warsh, Princeton University Press, 2021. JSTOR, https://doi.org/10.2307/j.ctv173f27w. Accessed 24 Nov. 2024.
